Jay Cutler Screen Crack

An experimentation with CSS3 animation that speaks to Jay Cutler's arm strength. Hover over the football for the effect. And wherever your volume is right now, cut it in half.

Barreling Ahead

My capstone project at Medill. Was tasked with producing a one-page, immersive, fully interactive story. The topic of choice was the craft beer industry. While all students were required to use Bootstrap as a base, I hand-coded the remainder of the site. Discovered the greatness of jQuery's smooth scroll, and can now hold my own in conversations with most beer snobs.

Cinemagraphs

A soon-to-be-completed compilation of the result of a period of time in which I became obsessed with cinemagraphs. Learned multiple techniques in both Adobe Photoshop and After Effects.

North by Northwestern Redesign (exp. completion 4/1)

A three-month project redesigning the website of the Northwestern's magazine, North By Northwestern. Extensive iterating and wireframing in InDesign/Photoshop/pen & paper for all device sizes, considerable typography research and hand-coding each page from scratch. A true learning experience.

My journalistic side

My extensive writing background includes positions (at) and work (for) a variety of prominent sports and lifestyle publications and websites, including UrbanDaddy (Boston & Chicago editions), The Improper Bostonian and Bleacher Report. I've been featured in USA Today, Yahoo! Sports and CBS, and currently write weekly columns for BaseballHQ.com. I did a bit of reporting at Medill as well.

About
